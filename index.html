Pinwheel DAO

A community-governed collective focused on ensuring the availability and permanence of public data

What is the Pinwheel DAO?

The Pinwheel DAO aims to incentivize the selection and pinning of public data sets to IPFS by rewarding DAO participants with governance rights and collectible tokens which grant additional community benefits, in turn, overcoming the shortfalls associated with decentralized storage incentivization models.
